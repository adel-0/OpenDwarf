# Demons — Know the Enemy

## What every demon is [wiki]
- Procedurally generated per world (subtypes: beast, flying spirit, humanoid,
  unique) — read its description; abilities vary hugely between worlds.
- **Immune to**: traps, pain, fear, nausea, stunning, exertion, dizziness,
  fevers, fire, all poisons. Fixed body temperature. No food/sleep/breath needs.
- **Enormous** (~167× dwarf body size), fast, strong, and start at *Accomplished*
  combat skill. Pain-based tactics (crippling limbs to disable) do NOT work —
  only structural damage (severing, pulping, brain/upper-body destruction) stops
  them.
- Many fly. Special attacks drawn from: webbing, fire breath, toxic spittle,
  poison gas/breath, syndrome-causing secretions. Fire/poison are survivable
  with full coverage armor and distance; **webbing is the worst** — webbed =
  helpless = dead. A webbing demon should be fled, not fought.

## Exploitable weaknesses [wiki]
- **Material demons**: some are made of weak substances (salt, snow, steam,
  mud, ash) — these can be destroyed by ordinary strong blows despite their
  size. Check the description; a "demon of steam" is a winnable fight far below
  the gear level a slade-world brute requires.
- Intelligent demons learn, but most demons rely on raw stats — a legendary
  dodger in a corridor/chokepoint denies the size advantage (they can't surround
  or flank). Fight 1-vs-1 in confined spaces, never in the open slade cavern.

## Unique demons (civilization rulers) [wiki]
- Humanoid-twisted demons that escaped through deity rituals; they rule goblin
  civilizations from dark-fortress throne rooms (top of the main tower).
- They are ancient and highly skilled, BUT: it is ONE demon, indoors, reachable
  through corridors, with no endless reinforcement wave — categorically easier
  than the underworld. **This is the intended first "kill a demon" target.**
- Expect the throne-room approach to aggro the tower garrison; sneak or fight
  through floors first; consider night infiltration.

## Engagement doctrine (policy seed)
- Never engage a demon: below 100% health, with any second hostile in range,
  near a pit edge, or with a webbing/unknown special attack and no escape route.
- Preconditions before any demon attempt: legendary-adjacent weapon skill +
  Dodger + Armor User, best-material full-coverage armor (steel minimum
  [prior]), a chokepoint plan, and a tested flee route.
- The win condition is one confirmed kill and extraction — not clearing.
