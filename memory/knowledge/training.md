# Getting Strong — Skill & Attribute Training Meta

All skills improve through use; the Reader skill is the ONE exception — it can
only be taken at character creation [wiki]. (Take Novice Reader at chargen if
the necromancy path is ever wanted — see `powers.md`.)

## The grind ladder (escalate only when the current tier is safe-boring)
1. **Harmless wildlife** (vermin, small animals): weapon-skill and Fighter XP
   with near-zero risk. Wrestling holds on harmless animals safely train
   Wrestler/Fighter [prior].
2. **Wolves, bandits, kobolds**: first real opponents; fight 1-at-a-time per
   policy.
3. **Night creature lairs, bandit camps**: named targets, fame, and loot.
4. **Beasts/ogres/were-creatures**: only with good armor and a flee plan.

## Defensive skills train by BEING attacked [prior — LIVE-VERIFY rates]
- **Dodger**: trains on successful dodges; **Armor User** on hits absorbed while
  armored; **Shield User** on blocks. They barely train on safe kills — they
  train in real fights against weak-but-numerous attackers.
- Classic method: wear full armor, fight low-damage enemies (e.g. unarmed/weak
  wildlife), let them swing. The policy must allow controlled multi-hit exposure
  for defense training while forbidding it for dangerous tiers.
- **Observer** is hard to train and matters (spot ambushes) [wiki]; it rises
  passively — don't burn time grinding it actively.

## Attributes
- Strength/agility/endurance/willpower rise from sustained use of skills that
  exercise them [prior]. Swimming and combat are attribute-rich. **Attribute
  gains stop forever if you become a necromancer** [wiki] — body first, slab
  later.

## Force multipliers
- **Throwing** is historically one of the strongest adventurer skills (train by
  throwing rocks; lethal at high levels) [prior — v0.53 balance UNVERIFIED].
- **Companions**: recruit hearthpersons/warriors via lords and taverns once fame
  permits; companions tank, plural beats skilled. Their deaths are acceptable
  losses (and postmortem material).
- **Sleep heals**: most wounds recover with rest; sleep indoors, repeatedly,
  before re-engaging. No sparring system exists in adventure mode [prior] —
  real (controlled) combat is the only teacher.
- **Swimmer**: train in SHALLOW water only until Adequate (Adequate swimmers
  don't drown while stunned [wiki]); deep water before that is a death sentence.
